CASE 01

Flavors Without Borders環遊食界環遊食界Flavors Without Borders

Embassy food-tasting YouTube series · TAITRA, Food Taipei Mega Shows 2025 · Role: concept, proposal, embassy outreach, pre-production大使館美食試吃 YouTube 系列 · TAITRA 台北國際食品系列展 2025 · 角色：企劃發想、提案、大使館洽談、前期製作

The challenge挑戰

Asia's largest food exhibition (1,700 exhibitors, 50,000+ visitors) needed international visibility beyond trade ads — something audiences would actually choose to watch.亞洲最大的食品展（1,700 家參展商、超過 50,000 名觀眾）需要的不只是廣告曝光，而是觀眾真正想主動觀看的內容。

What I did我做了什麼
  1. 1
    Wrote the format設計節目形式

    A host and embassy representatives taste-test the country's foods — stories, games, Q&A. 30–45 min shoots cut to ~7 min episodes.主持人與大使館代表一起試吃該國美食，穿插故事、遊戲與問答。30–45 分鐘的拍攝剪成約 7 分鐘一集。

  2. 2
    Pitched 10+ embassies — 6 committed提案 10+ 間大使館 — 6 間承諾參與

    Cold outreach to diplomatic missions in Taipei, including an in-person pitch at the Netherlands embassy.陌生開發台北的各國駐台機構，包含親自前往荷蘭在台辦事處提案。

  3. 3
    Secured host & production partner確定主持人與製作團隊

    Locked the on-camera host and video production company; defined the filming-day workflow.敲定鏡頭前主持人與影像製作公司，並規劃拍攝日的完整流程。

  4. 4
    Planned the rollout規劃上線節奏

    One episode every 1–2 weeks on YouTube, with teasers and behind-the-scenes on IG/FB via collab posts.每 1–2 週在 YouTube 上線一集，並以 IG/FB 合作貼文釋出前導與幕後花絮。

The outcome成果
5 episodes5 支影片published on the official channel於官方頻道上線
~60%pitch-to-commitment rate on cold embassy outreach大使館陌生開發的提案成功率

What pitching embassies taught me向大使館提案教會我的事

Lead with their outcome, not your content. Embassies didn't want to "be in a video" — they wanted their food culture in front of 50,000 visitors. Same idea, reframed, very different answer.先談對方的收穫，而不是你的內容。大使館要的不是「入鏡」，而是讓自己國家的飲食文化被 5 萬名觀眾看見。同一個想法，換個說法，答案完全不同。

CASE 02

Smart Manufacturing, Tasty Future智慧製造，美味未來

Factory-tour video series for international buyers · TAITRA, Food Taipei Mega Shows 2025 · Role: concept, proposal, format design拍給國際買主的工廠參訪影片系列 · TAITRA 台北國際食品系列展 2025 · 角色：企劃發想、提案、形式設計

The challenge挑戰

International buyers decide on Taiwanese suppliers without ever seeing how they work. How do you build trust in a factory from 8,000 km away?國際買主往往在沒看過工廠實際運作的情況下就要做出採購決定。要如何讓 8,000 公里外的人信任一座工廠？

What I did我做了什麼
  1. 1
    Proposed the series提出系列企劃

    Take buyers inside food, machinery, and packaging plants — tours, tastings, and production walk-throughs.帶買主走進食品、機械與包裝工廠 — 參訪、試吃、完整走一遍生產流程。

  2. 2
    Designed the format設計影片形式

    15–20 minute episodes, English-led with Chinese support, released ahead of the exhibition.每集 15–20 分鐘，英文為主、中文輔助，在展前陸續上線。

  3. 3
    Built in ESG & IoT storytelling融入 ESG 與 IoT 敘事

    Sustainability and smart production as the differentiator — matching the show's "Green Intelligence" theme.以永續與智慧製造作為差異化亮點，呼應展覽「綠色智慧」的年度主題。

The outcome成果
Adopted獲採用concept greenlit from my written proposal企劃書提案獲組織採納執行
3 episodes3 支影片published on the official channel於官方頻道上線

Honest footnote:誠實註記： filmed and published by the TAITRA team after my tenure.影片由 TAITRA 團隊在我離開後完成拍攝與上線。

CASE 03

A feed with a system有系統的版面

Instagram content system · @foodtaipeimegashows · Role: content design, visual system, bilingual copyInstagram 內容系統 · @foodtaipeimegashows · 角色：內容設計、視覺系統、雙語文案

The challenge挑戰

Five concurrent shows, dozens of exhibitor categories, one feed. Followers couldn't tell what any given post was about — or which show it belonged to.五個同期展覽、數十種參展商類別，卻只有一個帳號版面。粉絲很難一眼看出每則貼文在講什麼、屬於哪個展。

What I did我做了什麼
  1. 1
    Designed a color-coded system設計顏色分類系統

    One category color per post with bold, recognizable typography — a snack post and a packaging post are distinguishable at a glance.每個類別一個代表色，搭配辨識度高的粗體字 — 零食貼文和包裝貼文一眼就能分辨。

  2. 2
    Wrote everything twice每篇文案寫兩次

    English copy for international buyers, 中文 for local visitors — different hooks for each audience, not direct translation.英文寫給國際買主、中文寫給本地觀眾 — 針對不同受眾用不同切角，而不是直接翻譯。

  3. 3
    Ran the campaigns around it同步操作行銷活動

    30+ bilingual EDM campaigns driving visitor pre-registration; audience analysis with an external agency to map the B2B vs. B2C visitor mix.執行 30+ 檔雙語 EDM 推動觀眾預先登記，並與外部行銷公司合作受眾分析，釐清 B2B 與 B2C 的觀眾組成。

Field notes隨筆

How I think — in three short pieces我怎麼思考 — 三則短文
On pitching關於提案

Ten embassies, six yeses十間大使館，六個點頭

Diplomatic missions are the most cautious "clients" I've pitched. What moved them wasn't the video idea — it was opening every email with their goal: food culture in front of 50,000 visitors. Sell the outcome, and the format sells itself.駐台機構是我提案過最謹慎的「客戶」。打動他們的不是影片點子本身，而是每封信的開頭都先講他們的目標：讓自己國家的飲食文化被 5 萬名觀眾看見。先賣結果，形式自然水到渠成。

On bilingual copy關於雙語文案

Write it twice, differently寫兩次，寫得不一樣

Translation is not bilingual marketing. My English EDMs led with business value; the 中文 versions led with what's new and delicious. Same campaign, different cultural hooks — that's what "bilingual marketer" actually means.翻譯不等於雙語行銷。我的英文 EDM 開頭談商業價值，中文版開頭談有什麼新鮮好吃的。同一檔活動、不同的文化切角 — 這才是「雙語行銷人」真正的意思。

On premium brands關於精品品牌

Notes from 30,000 feet三萬英尺的筆記

Flying with Cathay Pacific is a daily study in brand delivery: a premium promise is kept or broken in hundreds of tiny, human moments. It changed how I think about "brand consistency" — it's operations, not just design.在國泰航空飛行，是每天都在上的品牌實踐課：精品承諾的成敗，取決於數百個細小的人與人瞬間。它改變了我對「品牌一致性」的理解 — 那是營運，不只是設計。
