The challenge挑戰

One of Asia's largest food exhibition (1,700 exhibitors, 50,000+ visitors) needed international visibility beyond trade ads —
something audiences would actually choose to watch. 亞洲最大的食品展之一（1,700 家參展商、超過 50,000 名觀眾），需要的不只是廣告曝光，而是觀眾真正想主動觀看的內容。

  2. 2
    Pitched 10+ embassies — 6 committed提案 10+ 間大使館 — 6 間承諾參與

    Cast a KOC host — on purpose刻意選擇 KOC 主持人

CASE 03

A feed with a system有系統的版面

Instagram content system · @foodtaipeimegashows
· Role: content design, visual system, bilingual copyInstagram 內容系統 · @foodtaipeimegashows

The challenge挑戰

Five concurrent shows, dozens of exhibitor categories, one feed. Followers couldn't tell what any given post was about — or which show it belonged to.五個子展覽、數十種參展商類別，卻只有一個帳號版面。追蹤者很難一眼看出每則貼文在講什麼、屬於哪個展。

What I did我做了什麼
  1. 1
    Designed a color-coded system設計顏色分類系統

    One category color per post with bold, recognizable typography — a snack post and a packaging post are distinguishable at a glance.每個類別一個代表色，搭配辨識度高的粗體字，不同主題一眼就能分辨。

  2. 2
    Wrote everything twice每篇文案寫兩次

    Ran the campaigns around it同步操作行銷活動

The question實驗問題

Could I grow an engaged audience from nothing, with no budget and no existing brand? A year-long experiment with every result measured.沒有預算、沒有既有品牌，我能不能從零養出一群高互動的粉絲？一場為期一年、每個結果都被記錄的實驗。

What I did我做了什麼
  1. 1
    Published on a fixed rhythm固定節奏發布

    Two Stories weekly — daily-life content plus weekly themes designed around interaction, not broadcast.每週兩則限時動態 — 日常分享加上每週主題，重點是互動，不是單向廣播。

    Collaborated with creators與創作者合作

What the failure taught me — and what I'd do next time失敗教會我的事 — 以及下次我會怎麼做

The giveaway hit 18.97% engagement but converted at 0.87% — attention and loyalty are different metrics. Next time I'd fix the funnel, not the prize: make the reward native to the brand (custom character stickers instead of a generic gift), set the entry action to follow + save rather than tag-only, and run a themed content series in the week after the draw to convert entrants while attention is still warm.抽獎的互動率有 18.97%，轉換率卻只有 0.87% — 注意力和忠誠度是兩個不同的指標。下次我會修的是漏斗，不是獎品：把獎品換成品牌原生的東西（原創角色貼圖，而不是通用贈品）、把參加條件從單純標記朋友改成追蹤＋收藏，並在開獎後一週推出主題系列內容，趁熱把參加者轉換成真正的粉絲。

On pitching關於提案

Ten embassies, six yeses十間大使館，六個點頭

Diplomatic missions are the most cautious "clients" I've pitched. What moved them wasn't the video idea — it was opening every email with their goal: food culture in front of 50,000 visitors. Sell the outcome, and the format sells itself.駐台機構是我提案過最謹慎的「客戶」。打動他們的不是影片點子本身，而是每封信的開頭都先講他們的目標：讓自己國家的飲食文化被台灣、海外民眾、買主看見。先賣結果，形式自然水到渠成。

On bilingual copy關於雙語文案

Write it twice, differently寫兩次，寫得不一樣

Translation is not bilingual marketing. My English EDMs led with business value; the 中文 versions led with what's new and delicious. Same campaign, different cultural hooks — that's what "bilingual marketer" actually means.翻譯不等於雙語行銷。我的英文 EDM 開頭談商業價值，中文版開頭談有什麼新鮮好吃的。同一檔活動、不同的文化切角 — 這才是「雙語行銷人」真正的意思。

On premium brands關於精品品牌

Notes from 30,000 feet三萬英尺的筆記

Flying with Cathay Pacific is a daily study in brand delivery: a premium promise is kept or broken in hundreds of tiny, human moments. It changed how I think about "brand consistency" — it's operations, not just design.在國泰航空飛行，是每天都在上的品牌實踐課：精品承諾的成敗，取決於數百個細小的人與人瞬間。它改變了我對「品牌一致性」的理解，那是營運，不只是設計。
